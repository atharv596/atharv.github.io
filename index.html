Our Story

Founded in 2020 by master baker Atharav Jadhav, Bakingo has quickly become Andheri's premier destination for artisanal baked goods. Our philosophy is simple: use the finest ingredients, traditional techniques, and a dash of innovation to create unforgettable flavors.

Every item in our bakery is handcrafted with meticulous attention to detail. We source our ingredients locally whenever possible, ensuring freshness and supporting our community.
